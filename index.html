About Us

Ash Fantastic Tombrown is dedicated to providing ready-made, nourishing, and healthy tombrown made from a wholesome blend of maize, soybean, groundnut, and dates. Rich in antioxidants, high in fiber, and supportive of gut health, our product is especially designed for health-conscious individuals, particularly those managing diabetes and avoiding sugar. We also cater to younger audiences who prefer a sweeter taste. To meet diverse preferences, our product comes in two variations: the date mix and the sugar mix.
